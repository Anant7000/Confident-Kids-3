About Us

Confident Kids is an international educational platform that empowers children with self-confidence and holistic development. Through online and offline programs, they offer a blend of academic and non-academic learning, nurturing intellectual, social, emotional, and moral growth. Their learner-centric approach emphasizes a secure, inclusive environment where students thrive through various activities. Confident Kids promotes 21st-century skills, fostering creativity, critical thinking, communication, and teamwork. Their dedicated team of experienced teachers fosters a positive and encouraging learning environment, ensuring each child receives individualized attention and support.

Founder

Ekta Pankaj Sevlani

Ekta Pankaj Sevlani, founder of Confident Kids, is a passionate educator dedicated to holistic child development. With years of experience in teaching and personality development coaching, she focuses on building self-confidence, leadership skills, and 21st-century skills in children. Her teaching philosophy emphasizes individual attention, creating a safe and encouraging environment for students to excel academically, socially, and emotionally. Ekta believes that confidence is a skill to be developed, and she empowers students to embrace their individuality and reach their full potential. Her award-winning approach blends academic learning with creative expression, ensuring a well-rounded education for every child
